✏️ Grammar
Textbook vs Real Native English
1. Contractions — Native တွေ အမြဲသုံးတယ်
❌ I am going to the store.
✅ I'm heading to the store.
ဆိုင်ကို သွားမယ်
❌ I do not know.
✅ I have no idea. / No clue.
မသိဘူး
❌ I am not able to come.
✅ I can't make it, sorry.
မလာနိုင်ဘူး
❌ I would like to ask you.
✅ I just wanted to ask...
မေးချင်တာလေး
2. Filler Words — Fluency ရဲ့ လျှို့ဝှက်ချက်
Well...
စပြောချိန် — "Well, I think it depends..."
I mean...
ရှင်းပြချိန် — "I mean, it's not simple."
You know?
အတည်ယူချိန် — "It's awkward, you know?"
Honestly...
Honest opinion — "Honestly, I loved it."
Actually...
မှန်ကန်တဲ့ info — "Actually, that's wrong."
3. Natural Expressions
❌ I am tired.
✅ I'm beat. / I'm wiped out.
❌ It is good.
✅ That's actually pretty good!
❌ I do not enjoy it.
✅ It's not really my thing.
❌ Can we speak later?
✅ Can we catch up later?
